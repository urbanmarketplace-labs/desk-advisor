How it works

Describe your current workspace in a few lines. Mention desk size, lighting, posture, clutter, cables, equipment and what feels inefficient. DeskLab will return a score, key issues, priority improvements and recommended upgrades.

1. Analyse the setup DeskLab identifies the highest-friction parts of your workspace based on what you describe.
2. Prioritise the fixes Get direct advice on what to change first before buying anything unnecessary.
3. Upgrade selectively Only introduce products where they solve a clear ergonomic, organisational or visual problem.

Analyse your workspace

Be specific. Stronger inputs produce more accurate diagnostics. Include what you use, what feels off, and what kind of setup you are trying to create.

Example: compact desk, laptop stand, poor evening lighting, visible cables, no storage, neck strain after long sessions
Current beta works from text input. Image-based analysis can be layered in later without changing the core recommendation engine.
